Our Services

We provide many exciting services that will leave you with a bright, white smile on your face!

Teeth Cleaning

We have a strong dental hygiene program with 2 excellent hygienists. Regular cleanings are one of the most important things you can do for your dental health.

Radiographs

We use digital x-ray which uses significantly less radiation.

Teeth Whitening

We offer take-home bleaching with custom trays or in office bleaching using the Zoom system.

Night Guards

Waking up with sore jaw muscles? Are your teeth showing excessive wear? A night guard may be the solution. It is a custom fabricated hard plastic device that fits on upper or lower teeth and protects them.

Pediatric Dentistry

At Cordata Dental we love taking care of kids' dental needs.  It is nice to have them come in for a "happy visit"  after age 1, this gets them used to the surroundings and even if they will not open initially, we like to see them every six months until we are able to clean and examine their teeth.  Sometimes this happens after 3 years of age.  Dr. Rosinski is also a proponent of fluoride drops or tablets to supplement tooth brushing.  We understand that ultimately it is  your choice to administer fluoride and we respect that.
